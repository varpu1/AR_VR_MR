Meuseums Currently Using AR, VR, or MR

Kennedy Space Center

The Heroes and Legends exhibit located at the Kennedy Space Center in Florida has a great use of augmented reality. You can go to some and actually see people talking bout themselves. One of the crown jewls of this exhibit is a hologram of Gene Cernan talking about and experiencing what his "Space walk from hell" was like.

Smithsonian National Museum of Natural History

Smithsonian National Museum of Natural History is located in Washington, D.C. In their "Bone Hall" they now have 13 skeletons which can be enhanced with 3-D augmented reality. If you get the smithsonisn's mobil app "Skin and Bones" then you experience flying vampire bats, and see what animals would look like with skin and muscles.
